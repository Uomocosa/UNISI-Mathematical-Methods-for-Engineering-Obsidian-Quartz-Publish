# General Solution
###### Useful Knowledge
$$
e^x = \sum_{n = 0}^{\infty} \frac{x^n}{n!} \kern30px \text{for} \ |x|>0
$$
<br>

###### General but long solution:
$$
\begin{align}
\large c_k  = \frac{1}{2\pi i} \oint_{\mathcal{C}[z_0, \ R]} \frac{f(z)}{z-z_0} \ dz
\\[10px]
\large f(z) = \sum_{k \ \in \ \mathbb{Z}} c_k \kern2px (z-z_0)^k
\end{align}
$$
