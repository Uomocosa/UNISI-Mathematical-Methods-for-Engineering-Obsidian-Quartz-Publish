### Def.: $L^p$ Spaces
A function $f(x)$ belongs to the $L^p(\mathbb{R})$ space (L : "Lebsgue" space) if:
$$
\large \int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}\left|f(x)\right|^p \ dx \ < \ \infty
$$
where $x \in \mathbb{R}$
And we can write : $f(x) \in L^p(\mathbb{R})$
